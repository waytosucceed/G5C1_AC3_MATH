In the bustling city of Numericton, where streets hummed with the rhythm of calculations, a curious youngster named Alex set out on a quest for knowledge.

Guided by the wise mentor, Professor Mathis, Alex delved into the complexities of the International System of Numeration.

Together, they navigated the intricacies of placing commas in large numbers, mastering the strategy to represent decimal values, and honing the skill of writing number names.
Through their journey, Alex gained a deeper understanding of the numerical tapestry that adorned the city, unlocking the secrets of its numerical wonders.
